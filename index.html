About VestlyFi

VestlyFi is a collection of free financial calculators built for people who want straight answers — not a sales pitch.

Most financial tools are either too complex, hidden behind a paywall, or designed to funnel you into a product. We built VestlyFi because we think everyone deserves access to the same math that financial advisors charge hundreds of dollars to explain.

Every calculator runs entirely in your browser. We never see the numbers you enter.

How VestlyFi Calculates

Core Approach
All calculators use standard financial formulas — compound interest, future value of annuities, and the 4% safe withdrawal rule. No proprietary black boxes.

The 4% Rule
Retirement projections use the 1998 Trinity Study's finding that a 4% annual withdrawal rate has a ~96% historical success rate over 30-year periods.

Inflation Adjustment
All projections use real (inflation-adjusted) returns. Dollar figures shown are in today's purchasing power.

For educational purposes only. Not financial advice. Consult a certified financial planner before making major financial decisions.

Privacy Policy

No Data Collected
VestlyFi does not collect, store, or transmit any financial information you enter. All calculations run entirely in your browser.

No Account Required
No email, no signup, no personal information needed.
