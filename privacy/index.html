Privacy Policy

Last updated March 2026

Overview

VestlyFi is a free financial tools site. We take your privacy seriously and aim to collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it's used.

What We Collect

For visitors using our calculators and diagram tools, we collect no personal information. These tools run entirely in your browser — nothing you enter is sent to our servers.

For users of the Net Worth Tracker, we collect:

  • Your email address, used to create and identify your account
  • Your password, stored as a secure hash — we never see it in plain text
  • The financial data you choose to log (asset and liability values and dates)

We also collect basic analytics data through Google Analytics, including pages visited, approximate location (country/region), browser type, and time on page. This data is aggregated and not tied to your identity.

How We Use It

  • Your email is used only to sign you in and send account-related messages (e.g. password resets)
  • Your financial data is used only to power your Net Worth Tracker — we do not analyze, share, or sell it
  • Analytics data is used to understand how people use the site so we can improve it

Data Storage & Security

Account and financial data is stored in Supabase, a managed database platform hosted on AWS. Access is protected by row-level security — meaning your data is only accessible to you when logged in with your account. No VestlyFi employee can access your financial data.

We use HTTPS on all pages. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt.

Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — site usage analytics
  • Supabase — account and data storage
  • Formspree — bug report form submissions

We do not sell your data to any third party, and we do not run advertising on VestlyFi.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your Account settings page. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, email us at vestlyfi@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of VestlyFi after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
